# **Exploding Kittens** 

Choose your feline and become a hero! Complete missions, unlock new skils, and ultimately face off against the military base intruder. 

    >**Select a cat!** Each cat has its respective abilities. 
    
    >**Collect artifacts and scrolls** from different locations and enemies!

    >**Save the military base!**

# **Back Story**

The Kitten Military Forces have defended their territory with precision and finesse. However, there has been a security breach detected at the main base!

You are a new recruit, ready to serve. The whole base is on lockdown and Sergeant Barking Kitten takes you under his paw! With a tiny meow, he demands you to:
    >"There's an intruder in our ranks, rookie! If you want to survive, you need to learn some skills fast!"
Your mission is clear: Find the Intruder and eliminate the threat!
